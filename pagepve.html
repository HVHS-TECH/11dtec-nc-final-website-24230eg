The cave was pretty deep and had a mine shaft in it but I didn't have a way to get out or the resources I wanted for my first big caving trip in my world. Then I went over the mountains to a village as it got dark. I slept there and then looted some food from them because I saw a cooler looking village where I wanted to make my base. I went to the cooler village and started making my base.
