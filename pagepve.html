Survival/PVE

About this page:

In this page I will explane how to do main line pregreshion in vanilia singel player survival, how to get the best tools and weapons, defeat the ender dragon and set your self up for a fantastic future in your world.

Start:

I spawned in a snowy taiga which is a decent spawn because it's not like in a desert or anything but there aren't any structures super close. I chopped down a tree and then dug like four blocks down to mine stone and crafted some basic stone tools. Then I started looking for a cave or some structures and found a igloo right beside a good cave. I looted the igloo which didn't have a basement so all I got was a bed and a furnace. Then I went into the cave and mined some copper to make full copper tools and armor whilst staying out of the dark. The cave was pretty deep and had a mine shaft in it but I didn't have a way to get out or the resources I wanted for my first big caving trip in my world. Then I went over the mountains to a village as it got dark. I slept there and then looted some food from them because I saw a cooler looking village where I wanted to make my base. I went to the cooler village and started making my base.

The mines:

I moved villages in to a hole in the side of a hill and broke and placed lecterns till one of them offered me a mending book which allows me to add an enchantment to any of my tools which makes them repair with XP so they are effectively immortal. Then I placed some chests for storing my loot and set my spawn. After that I went mining in a cave above my house in the mountain because iron is actually more common the higher you go. Then I go down the side of the cliff in to the ocean looking for a water cave because ores spawn smaller and less when exposed to air. I find one and follow it down and dig through a wall in to a mine shaft. I mine around the mine shaft till I have basically enough iron to make a full set of armor. Caving is a great opportunity to gather lots of drops from killing monsters without having to wait for night. Then I go deeper in to deepslate areas where diamonds spawn and I find a deep dark which is expected because they spawn under big mountains. The deep dark is also a good place to find diamonds because the sculk covers the walls so ores won't be exposed to air and sculk is very fast to mine with a hoe, but the deep dark is one of the most dangerous biomes in minecraft, so I go the other way. After I get around ten diamonds I decide to leave but as I'm caving upwards I find a monster spawner and get attacked by a bunch of mobs and I eventually get pushed off a ledge and die falling in lava which destroys all of my stuff. this is okay because I didn't bring all of my things with me and left some at my base, so I did the same thing again with but was more careful. I also mined obsidian, so I could go to the nether. I ended up leaving with more diamonds and stuff than I had when I died.

The nether:

I made a nether portal by making a five by four hollow square without the corners and lighting it with fire. Then I went through and came out in the nether floating over a sea of lava. I slowly bridged across and traded some of the gold I got from mining with the piglins and got some fire resistance. I saw a bastion and decided to loot it. Bastions are a difficult structure because they have piglin brutes which have fifty health and have axes which disable your shield, but the bastion is semi crucial to the progression of minecraft because the hold nether ite upgrade templates to max out your tools and have large amounts of gold which can ESIAL be traded with its inhabitants for ender pearls. The bastion I have is a treasure bastion which means there are guaranteed nether ite upgrade templates in the bottom chest. I pot fire resistance and go in through the bottom part. I don't know how to rout a bastion let alone a modern one so after looting two chests stealing lots of gold, running out of fire resistance and getting a few close calls with piglin brutes I leave and go home. Then I go back to the nether to trade with some piglins for ender pearls and other things. Then I return home once more and do some work around farming and stuff.
